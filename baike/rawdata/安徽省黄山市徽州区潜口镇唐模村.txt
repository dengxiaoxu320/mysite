<div class="para">乐善堂北面毗连着“<a target=_blank href="/view/342127.htm">曹门厅</a>”，建于明嘉靖三十三年（1554年），原为潜口镇汪曹（其先人曾任宋代漕运使）后裔支祠，此厅檐罩高悬，九开间的门庭一字形展开，八根献柱整齐划列，庄严肃穆。建筑宏伟，为一般视祠堂所不及。</div>
<div class="para">司谏第</div>
<div class="para">穿过曹门厅门前石坪顺势而下，有“司谏第”，始建于明弘治八年<div class="text_pic layoutright" data-layout="right" style="width:220px;padding-left: 20px"><a href="/picview/1577747/1577747/0/4075890abf60372195ca6bc9.html?fr=lemma" class="lemmapic-mask use-picture-dialog" target="_blank" style="width:220px;height:165px;left: 20px"

<div class="para"><a target=_blank href="/view/343126.htm">方观田宅</a></div>
<div class="para">位于山庄北侧，始建于明中叶，原坐落歙县坑日乡沦潭村，是一座明代<a target=_blank href="/view/13575.htm">徽州</a>普通农民住宅，该宅为一进二层三间两厢式，小青瓦、<a target=_blank href="/view/206341.htm">马头墙</a>、楼厅、<a target=_blank href="/view/120282.htm">天井</a>、<a target=_blank href="/view/2426231.htm">栏板</a>均采用护缝制装饰，楼下青砖铺地，大门有门罩保护、门内罩饰有霸王拳，专家认为此宅是徽州普通农民住宅的代表。</div><div class="anchor-list"><a name="9" class="anchor-1"></a><a name="sub1577747_9" class="anchor-1"></a><a name="旅游指南" class="anchor-1"></a></div><h2 class="headline-1"><span class="headline-1-index">9</span><span class="headline-content">旅游指南</span><span class="text_edit editable-title" style="margin-left: 10px;" data-edit-id="2329050:%E6%BD%9C%E5%8F%A3%E9%95%87:9"><a href="#" class="nslog:1019" title="编辑本段">编辑</a></span></h2><div class="para"></div>
